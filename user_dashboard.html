About Us

Welcome to the Vehicle Tracking System! Our goal is to improve the daily commute experience for SGGSIE&T students and staff by providing a reliable and efficient way to track buses in real-time.

This system was built with the following key benefits in mind:

  • Time Optimization: Real-time tracking helps save time by providing accurate ETAs.
  • Fuel Efficiency: Prevents unnecessary delays and optimizes routes, saving fuel.
  • Class Session Attendance: Reduces the chance of missing important sessions due to transport delays.
  • Notifications: Stay updated with SMS or email alerts on the bus's status.

Our goal is to help students and staff efficiently manage their daily commute and ensure timely arrivals.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: How can I track the bus in real-time?

You can easily track the bus by logging into your dashboard. It will display the bus's location on a live map, along with an accurate ETA to your destination.

Q2: What happens if the bus is delayed?

If the bus is delayed, you will receive real-time notifications on your mobile device or email. The system will update the ETA automatically based on the bus's current location.

Q3: How do I enable notifications?

You can enable notifications from the settings section of your dashboard. Choose to receive alerts via SMS or email based on your preference.

Q4: Can I track multiple buses?

Yes, the system allows you to track multiple buses at once. You can easily toggle between different buses to check their location and status.
